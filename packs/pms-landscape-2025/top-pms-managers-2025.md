# Top PMS Managers — 2025 Analysis
**Last updated**: 2025-03-25
**Source**: SEBI PMS disclosures, PMS Bazaar, industry reports
**Target agent**: pms-analyst

## Market Overview

- Total PMS AUM (industry): ~Rs 32L Cr (as of Dec 2024)
- Number of SEBI-registered PMS: ~400+
- Minimum investment: Rs 50 Lakhs
- Typical HNI allocation to PMS: 20-40% of equity portfolio

## Top PMS Managers

### 1. Marcellus Investment Managers
- **Strategy**: Consistent Compounders (CCP), Kings of Capital (KoC), Rising Giants
- **Philosophy**: Quality + clean management + capital allocation. Buy and hold forever.
- **AUM**: ~Rs 12,000 Cr
- **Key Holdings (CCP)**: Asian Paints, Pidilite, HDFC Bank, Bajaj Finance, TCS
- **Fee**: 1% fixed + 20% performance above 10% hurdle
- **Track Record**: CCP ~20% CAGR since 2018 (pre-fee)
- **Strengths**: Research depth, forensic accounting focus, capital allocation framework
- **Risks**: Concentrated (15-20 stocks), quality premium can compress, high valuation holdings

### 2. ASK Investment Managers
- **Strategy**: Growth, Indian Entrepreneur (IEP), Select
- **AUM**: ~Rs 8,000 Cr
- **Philosophy**: Growth at reasonable price (GARP), entrepreneur-led businesses
- **Key Holdings**: ICICI Bank, L&T, Kotak Bank, Infosys, SBI
- **Fee**: 1.5% fixed + 15% performance above 8% hurdle (varies by strategy)
- **Track Record**: IEP ~18% CAGR since inception
- **Strengths**: Long track record, institutional processes
- **Risks**: Some strategy overlap with large-cap MFs

### 3. 360 ONE (formerly IIFL) Asset Management
- **Strategy**: Multicap, Special Opportunities, BAF
- **AUM**: ~Rs 15,000 Cr (across PMS + AIF)
- **Philosophy**: Multi-strategy approach, event-driven opportunities
- **Fee**: 1.5-2% fixed + 15-20% performance
- **Strengths**: Wide product range, strong distribution
- **Risks**: Complex fee structures, need to evaluate each strategy separately

### 4. Motilal Oswal PMS
- **Strategy**: Next Trillion Dollar Opportunity (NTDO), Value, Business Superheroes
- **AUM**: ~Rs 6,000 Cr
- **Philosophy**: QGLP (Quality, Growth, Longevity, Price)
- **Key Holdings (NTDO)**: HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Bajaj Finance, M&M, L&T
- **Fee**: 2% fixed OR 1% fixed + 15% performance
- **Track Record**: NTDO ~16% CAGR since 2007
- **Strengths**: QGLP framework, large team, brand
- **Risks**: Large AUM can limit nimbleness, benchmark-like returns in some periods

### 5. Alchemy Capital
- **Strategy**: High Growth, Ascent
- **AUM**: ~Rs 3,500 Cr
- **Philosophy**: Multi-cap with growth bias
- **Fee**: 1.5% fixed + 20% above hurdle
- **Track Record**: Alchemy HG ~22% CAGR since inception
- **Strengths**: Nimble, concentrated portfolios, strong in midcaps
- **Risks**: Higher volatility, key person risk

### 6. Unifi Capital
- **Strategy**: BCAD (Blended Cross-Asset Dynamic), Think India
- **AUM**: ~Rs 2,500 Cr
- **Philosophy**: Cross-asset allocation, Blended approach
- **Fee**: Performance-only model (no fixed fee in some strategies)
- **Strengths**: Unique fee structure, cross-asset framework
- **Risks**: Smaller team, less institutional infrastructure

### 7. Avendus PMS
- **Strategy**: Alpha Fund, Factor Fund
- **AUM**: ~Rs 4,000 Cr
- **Philosophy**: Factor-based investing, absolute return
- **Fee**: 1% fixed + 20% performance
- **Strengths**: Quantitative approach, risk management focus
- **Risks**: Factor strategies can underperform in momentum markets

### 8. Invesco India PMS
- **Strategy**: Multicap, Largecap
- **Philosophy**: Global institutional process, fundamental bottom-up
- **Fee**: 1-1.5% fixed
- **Strengths**: Global research backing, institutional discipline
- **Risks**: Conservative approach may lag in strong bull markets

### 9. ICICI Prudential PMS
- **Strategy**: Value, Contra, Flexicap
- **AUM**: ~Rs 5,000 Cr
- **Fee**: 1.5% fixed
- **Strengths**: Large brand, conservative risk management
- **Risks**: Bureaucratic decision-making, overlap with MF schemes

### 10. Vallum Capital
- **Strategy**: India Discovery
- **AUM**: ~Rs 800 Cr
- **Philosophy**: Deep value, special situations, turnarounds
- **Fee**: 0% fixed + 25% performance above 15% hurdle
- **Track Record**: Outstanding long-term performance
- **Strengths**: True performance-aligned fee, deep value focus
- **Risks**: Volatile returns, illiquid positions, concentrated portfolio

## Selection Framework

| Criteria | Weight | What to Check |
|---------|--------|--------------|
| Track record (5yr+) | 30% | CAGR, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, consistency |
| Investment philosophy | 20% | Alignment with your beliefs, clarity of process |
| Fee structure | 15% | Total cost including fixed + performance + exit load |
| Team stability | 15% | Key person dependency, team turnover |
| AUM size | 10% | Too small (<500Cr) = risky, Too large (>15KCr) = index-like |
| Transparency | 10% | Reporting frequency, portfolio disclosure, communication |
