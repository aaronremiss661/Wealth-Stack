# TDS / TCS Rates on Investments — FY 2025-26
**Last updated**: 2025-03-25
**Source**: Income Tax Act, Sections 192-206C
**Target agent**: tax-strategist

## TDS on Investment Income (Residents)

| Income Type | Section | Threshold | TDS Rate |
|------------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Dividends | 194 | Rs 5,000/yr | 10% |
| Interest on securities | 193 | Rs 10,000/yr | 10% |
| Bank FD interest | 194A | Rs 40,000/yr (50K for seniors) | 10% |
| Rent (individual paying) | 194IB | Rs 50,000/month | 5% |
| Property sale | 194IA | Rs 50,00,000 | 1% |
| MF capital gains (>1Cr) | 194F | Rs 1,00,00,000 | — |
| Insurance commission | 194D | Rs 15,000/yr | 5% |
| Lottery/gaming | 194B/194BB | Rs 10,000 | 30% |

## TCS (Tax Collected at Source)

| Transaction | Section | Threshold | TCS Rate |
|------------|---------|-----------|----------|
| LRS remittance (education with loan) | 206C(1G) | Rs 7,00,000/FY | 0.5% |
| LRS remittance (education, no loan) | 206C(1G) | Rs 7,00,000/FY | 5% |
| LRS remittance (other) | 206C(1G) | Rs 7,00,000/FY | 20% |
| Foreign tour package | 206C(1G) | Rs 7,00,000/FY | 5% (up to 7L) / 20% (above) |
| Sale of goods | 206C(1H) | Rs 50,00,000/FY | 0.1% |

**Important**: TCS on LRS was reduced from previous higher rates. The Rs 7L threshold applies per financial year across all purposes.

## TDS Credit and Refund

- TDS is advance tax — claim credit while filing ITR
- If TDS exceeds actual tax liability, claim refund
- Track via Form 26AS / AIS (Annual Information Statement)
- TDS certificates: Form 16 (salary), Form 16A (non-salary)
- Verify TDS credits match between Form 26AS and Form 16A before filing

## Planning Tips

1. **Submit 15G/15H** early in the FY to avoid unnecessary TDS on FD interest
2. **Split FDs across banks** to stay below Rs 40K threshold per bank
3. **NRIs**: Apply for lower TDS certificate (Section 197) if DTAA rate is lower
4. **TCS on LRS**: Claim credit in ITR — it's not an additional tax, just advance collection
5. **Dividend income**: Plan for TDS impact on cash flow — 10% deducted at source
