# Capital Gains Tax Rules — FY 2025-26
**Last updated**: 2025-03-25
**Source**: Finance (No. 2) Act, 2024
**Target agent**: tax-strategist

## Key Changes (July 2024 Budget)

1. **Equity LTCG rate**: Increased from 10% to **12.5%**
2. **Equity STCG rate**: Increased from 15% to **20%**
3. **LTCG exemption**: Increased from Rs 1L to **Rs 1.25L** per year
4. **Property**: Indexation benefit removed, flat 12.5% LTCG
5. **Debt MF**: Continue to be taxed at slab rate (no LTCG benefit, since April 2023)
6. **Holding period for unlisted**: Reduced from 36 to 24 months for LTCG

## Complete Rate Card

| Asset | Holding for LTCG | LTCG Rate | STCG Rate |
|-------|-----------------|-----------|-----------|
| Listed equity shares | >12 months | 12.5% above Rs 1.25L | 20% |
| Equity MF (>65% equity) | >12 months | 12.5% above Rs 1.25L | 20% |
| Debt MF | N/A (no LTCG) | At slab rate | At slab rate |
| Hybrid MF (35-65% equity) | >24 months | 12.5% | At slab rate |
| Gold MF/ETF | >24 months | 12.5% | At slab rate |
| Listed bonds/debentures | >12 months | 12.5% | At slab rate |
| Unlisted shares | >24 months | 12.5% | At slab rate |
| Real estate | >24 months | 12.5% (no indexation) | At slab rate |
| SGB (held to maturity) | 8 years | **Exempt** | N/A |
| SGB (sold on exchange) | >12 months | 12.5% | At slab rate |

## Grandfathering (Equity)

- For equity held before Jan 31, 2018: cost of acquisition = higher of (actual cost, fair market value on 31-Jan-2018)
- For property bought before July 23, 2024: taxpayer can choose between (old rate with indexation) or (12.5% without indexation), whichever is lower

## Set-Off and Carry Forward

- STCL can be set off against both STCG and LTCG
- LTCL can be set off only against LTCG
- Carry forward for 8 assessment years
- Must file ITR by due date to carry forward losses

## STT Rates (FY 2025-26)

| Transaction | Rate | Paid by |
|-------------|------|---------|
| Equity delivery buy | 0.1% | Buyer |
| Equity delivery sell | 0.1% | Seller |
| Equity intraday sell | 0.025% | Seller |
| Futures sell | 0.0125% | Seller |
| Options sell | 0.1% (on premium) | Seller |
| MF equity redemption | 0.001% | Seller |

Note: Options STT increased significantly from 0.0625% to 0.1% effective Oct 2024.

## Tax Harvesting Strategy

- Harvest LTCG up to Rs 1.25L per year tax-free
- Book losses before March 31 to carry forward
- Avoid wash sale: don't repurchase same security within 30 days
- For MF: switch between similar funds (e.g., Nifty 50 → Nifty Next 50) to book loss without losing exposure
