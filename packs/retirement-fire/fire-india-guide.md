# FIRE Planning for India
**Last updated**: 2025-03-25
**Source**: Indian FIRE community research, actuarial data
**Target agent**: retirement-specialist

## FIRE Number Calculation

**Basic Formula**: Annual Expenses × Multiplier

| FIRE Type | Multiplier | Safe Withdrawal Rate | Description |
|-----------|-----------|---------------------|-------------|
| Lean FIRE | 25x | 4.0% | Bare minimum — frugal lifestyle |
| Regular FIRE | 30x | 3.3% | Comfortable lifestyle |
| Fat FIRE | 40x | 2.5% | Luxurious lifestyle |
| Coast FIRE | N/A | N/A | Enough invested to coast to retirement age |
| Barista FIRE | N/A | N/A | Part-time work covers expenses, investments grow |

### India-Specific Adjustments

The US "4% rule" (Trinity study) needs adjustment for India:
- **Higher inflation**: India's long-term inflation is 6-7% vs US 2-3%
- **Higher returns**: Indian equity returns are higher (12-14% nominal vs 8-10% US)
- **Healthcare costs**: No Medicare equivalent — budget 15-20% for health costs
- **Longer retirement**: If retiring at 40, need 45-50 years of funding

**Recommended Indian safe withdrawal rate**: **3.0-3.5%** (multiplier: 28-33x)

## Expense Budgeting for FIRE

### Must-Include
- **Health insurance**: Rs 50K-1.5L/yr for family (increases with age — budget 10% annual increase)
- **Property tax & maintenance**: If own house
- **Inflation buffer**: Add 20% to current expenses for unexpected inflation
- **Child education**: If children not yet independent
- **Parent care**: Medical expenses for aging parents

### Often Forgotten
- **Vehicle replacement**: Every 8-10 years
- **Home renovation**: Every 10-15 years
- **Gadget replacement**: Phone/laptop every 3-5 years
- **Travel**: Post-FIRE, people travel more
- **Hobby/skill costs**: Classes, equipment, subscriptions

## Asset Allocation for FIRE

### Accumulation Phase (Pre-FIRE)
| Age | Equity | Debt | Gold |
|-----|--------|------|------|
| 25-35 | 80% | 15% | 5% |
| 35-40 | 70% | 25% | 5% |
| 40-45 | 60% | 30% | 10% |

### Withdrawal Phase (Post-FIRE)
| Years into FIRE | Equity | Debt | Gold/Cash |
|----------------|--------|------|-----------|
| 0-5 | 50% | 40% | 10% |
| 5-15 | 50% | 35% | 15% |
| 15+ | 40% | 40% | 20% |

Key: Maintain equity allocation even in retirement — you need growth to beat inflation over 40+ years.

## Tax Optimization for FIRE

1. **Use LTCG exemption**: Harvest Rs 1.25L equity LTCG annually (tax-free)
2. **New regime**: With low/no salary income, new regime with Rs 12L rebate is powerful
3. **SWP from equity MF**: Systematic Withdrawal Plan — only gains portion is taxable
4. **Debt MF SWP**: Taxed at slab rate — use when in lower tax bracket post-FIRE
5. **NPS annuity**: 40% annuity is taxable — minimize by choosing low annuity option
6. **HUF**: Separate tax entity with its own slab — useful for FIRE families
