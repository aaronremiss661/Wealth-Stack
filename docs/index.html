CAPABILITIES

Everything your wealth advisor does

Each skill is a domain expert. Tax harvesting, NRI repatriation, PMS due diligence — ask it anything.

Portfolio Analysis

Holdings breakdown, XIRR, sector exposure, concentration risk. Import from Zerodha, Groww, or CSV.

/wealthstack:portfolio

Tax Optimization

LTCG at 12.5%, STCG at 20%. Wash sale detection, lot-level optimization, HUF exemption stacking.

/wealthstack:tax-optimize

PMS & AIF

Evaluate portfolio management services and alternative investment funds. Fee analysis, tax drag, suitability.

/wealthstack:pms-aif

NRI Wealth

US, UAE, UK, Singapore corridors. FEMA, DTAA, PFIC traps, NRE/NRO structuring, repatriation.

/wealthstack:nri-wealth

Goal-Based Planning

Map every rupee to a life goal. Glide path allocation shifts automatically as goals approach.

/wealthstack:goal-planner

Family Office

Trusts, HUF, holding companies, succession, philanthropy, governance. For Rs 100Cr+ families.

/wealthstack:family-office

Risk Assessment

Sharpe, Sortino, VaR, max drawdown. Stress test against 2008, COVID, demonetization scenarios.

/wealthstack:risk-assess

SEBI Compliance

Suitability analysis, concentration limits, KYC audit, documentation checklist, regulatory tracking.

/wealthstack:compliance

Client Reports

Professional HTML reports with performance attribution, risk dashboard, and recommendations.

/wealthstack:client-report
