## Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGB)
- Issued by RBI on behalf of Government of India
- Interest: 2.5% p.a. on issue price (paid semi-annually, taxable at slab)
- Tenure: 8 years, exit option after 5th year (on interest payment dates)
- LTCG at maturity: **Completely tax-exempt** (biggest advantage)
- LTCG if sold on exchange before maturity: 12.5%
- No new issuances since Feb 2024 — secondary market only
- Premium on secondary market: 5-10% above gold price (reflecting tax benefit)

## Recommended Allocation
- 5-15% of portfolio in gold (hedge against equity/INR)
- Preference: SGB (if available at reasonable premium) > Gold ETF > Gold MF
- Avoid physical gold for investment (high making charges, storage, purity risk)
