## Key Facts
- Minimum lot size: 1 unit (reduced from earlier higher lots)
- Listing: NSE/BSE, traded like stocks
- Distribution: Mandatory 90% of net distributable cash flow
- Tax: Distributions partly tax-free (return of capital), partly interest (slab), partly dividend (slab)
- LTCG: 12.5% (holding >12 months for listed), STCG: at slab rate (previously 15%, now slab post-2024)
- SEBI mandates: Minimum 80% completed assets, leverage cap 49% of assets
- Suitable for: Income seekers, portfolio diversification, real estate exposure without direct ownership
