# Treasury Instruments — Indian Money Market

## Money Market Instruments Overview

| Instrument | Issuer | Tenor | Min Investment | Yield (approx.) |
|-----------|--------|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| Treasury Bills (T-Bills) | RBI / Govt of India | 91/182/364 days | Rs 25,000 | 6.5-6.8% |
| Commercial Paper (CP) | Corporates | 7 days - 1 year | Rs 5 lakh | 7.0-8.5% |
| Certificate of Deposit (CD) | Banks | 7 days - 1 year | Rs 1 lakh | 6.5-7.5% |
| TREPS | Market participants | Overnight/term | Rs 5 Cr | 6.0-6.5% |
| Repo (RBI) | RBI | Overnight | Institutional | 6.25% (repo rate) |
| Government Securities (G-Sec) | Govt of India | 5-40 years | Rs 10,000 | 6.8-7.5% |
| State Development Loans (SDL) | State govts | 10 years typical | Rs 10,000 | 7.0-7.8% |

## TREPS (Tri-Party Repo)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Full form | Tri-Party Repo Dealing and Settlement |
| Operator | CCIL (Clearing Corporation of India) |
| Replaced | CBLO (Collateralised Borrowing and Lending Obligation) in 2018 |
| Collateral | Government securities, T-Bills |
| Tenor | Overnight to 1 year |
| Participants | Banks, MFs, insurance companies, PDs, corporates (via clearing members) |
| Rate | Typically between SDF rate (6.00%) and repo rate (6.25%) |
| Settlement | T+0 for overnight |
| Use | Liquid funds invest heavily in TREPS for overnight deployment |

## Commercial Paper (CP)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Issuer eligibility | Companies with A2 or above credit rating |
| Minimum size | Rs 5 lakh (per issue) |
| Denomination | Multiples of Rs 5 lakh |
| Issued at | Discount to face value |
| Tenor | 7 days to 1 year |
| Rating | Must be rated by SEBI-registered CRA |
| Settlement | T+1 |
| Current yields | AAA CP (3-month): 7.0-7.5%, AA+ CP: 7.5-8.0% |

### CP Risk Assessment
- Default risk: Higher than G-Sec or bank CD
- Liquidity risk: Secondary market exists but not always deep
- Recent defaults: IL&FS (2018), DHFL (2019) — rattled CP market
- Mutual funds: Liquid funds can hold up to 20% in single issuer's CP (SEBI limit)

## Certificate of Deposit (CD)

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Issuer | Scheduled commercial banks |
| Minimum size | Rs 1 lakh (per issue) |
| Denomination | Multiples of Rs 1 lakh |
| Tenor | 7 days to 1 year (banks), 1-3 years (financial institutions) |
| Issued at | Discount to face value |
| Pre-mature withdrawal | Not allowed (but can be sold in secondary market) |
| Settlement | T+1 |
| Current yields | 6.5-7.5% depending on issuing bank and tenor |

## Government Securities (G-Sec) — For Corporate Treasury

| Instrument | Tenor | Yield | Suitable For |
|-----------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 91-day T-Bill | 3 months | 6.5% | Ultra-short parking |
| 182-day T-Bill | 6 months | 6.6% | Short-term surplus |
| 364-day T-Bill | 1 year | 6.7% | Annual surplus |
| 5Y G-Sec | 5 years | 6.9% | Medium-term allocation |
| 10Y G-Sec benchmark | 10 years | 7.0-7.2% | ALM matching |

### RBI Retail Direct (for smaller corporates / HNIs)
- Platform: RBI Retail Direct portal (online)
- No demat account needed (maintained by RBI)
- Bid in primary auction (non-competitive)
- Minimum: Rs 10,000 (G-Sec), Rs 25,000 (T-Bill)
- Zero brokerage, zero custody charge

## Corporate Treasury Investment Policy Framework

### Liquidity Tiers

| Tier | Purpose | Instruments | Return Target |
|------|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Tier 1: Operational (0-7 days) | Day-to-day payments | Bank balance, overnight fund | 5.5-6.0% |
| Tier 2: Tactical (7-90 days) | Short-term surplus | Liquid fund, TREPS, T-Bills | 6.5-7.0% |
| Tier 3: Strategic (90-365 days) | Planned deployment | Ultra-short fund, CP, CD | 7.0-7.5% |
| Tier 4: Core (1-3 years) | Long-term surplus | Short duration fund, SDL, G-Sec | 7.0-8.0% |

## Tax Treatment (Corporate)

| Instrument | Tax Treatment |
|-----------|--------------|
| FD interest | 25% corporate tax rate (Sec 115BAA) |
| MF (debt) gains | Slab rate (no indexation post-2023) |
| MF (equity) LTCG | 12.5% (if held >12 months — rare for treasury) |
| T-Bill discount | Taxed as interest income |
| CP/CD discount | Taxed as interest income |
| G-Sec coupon | Taxed as interest income |
| Dividend from MF | Corporate slab rate |
