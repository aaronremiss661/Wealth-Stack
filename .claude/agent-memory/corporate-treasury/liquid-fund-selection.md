# Liquid Fund Selection & Treasury Management

## Liquid Fund vs Overnight Fund

| Feature | Liquid Fund | Overnight Fund |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| Max maturity of holdings | 91 days | 1 day (overnight) |
| Typical yield | 6.5-7.0% | 6.0-6.5% |
| Exit load | Graded (1-7 days), nil after 7 days | Nil |
| NAV application | Same-day (before 1:30 PM) | Same-day (before 1:30 PM) |
| Credit risk | Low (but CP/CD exposure) | Negligible (TREPS, repo) |
| Interest rate risk | Very low | Near zero |
| Use case | Surplus for 7-90 days | Surplus for 1-7 days |
| SEBI rules | Mark-to-market from 2019 | Mark-to-market |

## Liquid Fund Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What to Check |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Credit quality | 30% | % in AAA/A1+, sovereign, TREPS |
| AUM size | 20% | Larger = more stable (>Rs 10,000 Cr preferred) |
| Expense ratio (direct) | 15% | Lower is better (0.08-0.20%) |
| Yield consistency | 15% | Rolling 30-day yield stability |
| AMC reputation | 10% | Track record, debt fund history |
| Concentration | 10% | Single issuer exposure (SEBI limit: 20%) |

## SEBI Regulations for Liquid Funds

| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Mark-to-market | All securities must be marked to market (no amortization) |
| Single issuer limit | Max 20% in single issuer (15% for lower-rated) |
| Sector limit | Max 20% in single sector (for non-TREPS) |
| Min 20% liquid assets | Must hold min 20% in cash, T-Bills, G-Sec, TREPS |
| No structured obligations | Cannot invest in structured deposits or credit-enhanced paper |
| No unlisted securities | Cannot invest in unlisted debt |
| Swing pricing | SEBI has introduced swing pricing for debt funds (under implementation) |

## Corporate Treasury Management Best Practices

### Cash Forecasting
1. Maintain 13-week rolling cash forecast
2. Segregate: Operational cash needs vs investable surplus
3. Buffer: Keep 10-15% more than projected need in Tier 1
4. Review weekly, adjust investments accordingly

### Investment Approval Framework
| Amount | Approval Level |
|--------|---------------|
| Up to Rs 10 Cr | Treasury manager |
| Rs 10-50 Cr | CFO |
| Rs 50 Cr+ | Board / Investment Committee |

### Risk Controls
1. **Counterparty limits**: Max 20% per issuer (aligned with SEBI)
2. **Duration limit**: Average portfolio maturity < 91 days for liquid, < 30 days for overnight
3. **Credit floor**: Minimum A1+ / AAA rated instruments
4. **Concentration**: No more than 30% in any single AMC
5. **Benchmark**: Target 91-day T-Bill yield + 25-50 bps
6. **Review**: Monthly investment committee review

### Decision Framework
- **Idle cash <7 days**: Overnight fund
- **Idle cash 7-30 days**: Liquid fund
- **Idle cash 30-90 days**: Ultra-short duration fund
- **Idle cash >90 days**: Low duration or short-term debt fund
- **Known outflow date**: FD or target maturity fund matching the date
