## Volatility Index

| Index | Description | Interpretation |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| India VIX | Implied volatility from Nifty options | Fear gauge of Indian market |

**India VIX Interpretation**:
- < 12: Very low fear, complacency (potential risk of sharp moves)
- 12-15: Normal, calm markets
- 15-20: Elevated uncertainty
- 20-30: High fear, significant uncertainty (election, budget, global crisis)
- > 30: Panic levels (COVID peak hit ~84 in Mar 2020)
- > 50: Extreme panic (rare, crisis-level)

## Index Rebalancing

- **Nifty 50**: Semi-annual (March and September)
- **Other NSE indices**: Semi-annual rebalancing
- **AMFI market cap classification**: Updated every January and July
- Rebalancing creates short-term trading opportunities (inclusion/exclusion effect)

## FII / DII Flow Impact

| Flow Direction | Market Impact |
|---------------|---------------|
| FII buying + DII buying | Strong rally |
| FII buying + DII selling | Moderate up (FII dominant in large cap) |
| FII selling + DII buying | Range-bound to modest decline |
| FII selling + DII selling | Sharp correction |

- FII ownership in NSE-listed stocks: ~17-18% (declining from 23% in 2015)
- DII ownership: ~16-17% (increasing, driven by MF SIP flows)
- DII monthly net flows (MF SIPs): ~Rs 25,000 Cr/month (structural floor)
