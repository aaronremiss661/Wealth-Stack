# Market Calendar & Event Schedule

## RBI Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) Schedule

MPC meets 6 times a year (bi-monthly). Each meeting is 3 days, decision on last day.

### Typical Annual MPC Schedule:
| Meeting | Months | Key Focus |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| 1st | February | Post-budget assessment |
| 2nd | April | Annual monetary policy review |
| 3rd | June | Monsoon outlook, Q4 GDP |
| 4th | August | Monsoon update, inflation data |
| 5th | October | Festival season, H1 review |
| 6th | December | Year-end review, global outlook |

### Current Policy Rates (FY 2025-26)
| Rate | Current Level |
|------|---------------|
| Repo Rate | 6.25% (cut from 6.50% in Feb 2025) |
| Standing Deposit Facility (SDF) | 6.00% |
| Marginal Standing Facility (MSF) | 6.50% |
| Bank Rate | 6.50% |
| CRR | 4.00% |
| SLR | 18.00% |
| Reverse Repo | 3.35% (effectively replaced by SDF) |

## Corporate Earnings Season Calendar

| Quarter | Period | Results Season | Key Sectors First |
|---------|--------|---------------|------------------|
| Q1 | Apr-Jun | July-August | IT (first), then BFSI, rest |
| Q2 | Jul-Sep | October-November | IT, BFSI, auto |
| Q3 | Oct-Dec | January-February | IT, BFSI, FMCG |
| Q4 | Jan-Mar | April-May | IT, BFSI, entire market |

### Earnings Season Patterns
- IT companies report first (TCS/Infosys in first 2 weeks)
- Banking results drive Bank Nifty moves
- Auto sales data: Released 1st of every month
- Results impact: Maximum price movement on result day +/- 1 day

## Key Annual Events

| Event | Typical Timing | Market Impact |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| Union Budget | Feb 1 | High — tax, capex, fiscal deficit |
| Economic Survey | Jan 31 | Moderate — sets expectations |
| Advance tax payment deadlines | Jun 15, Sep 15, Dec 15, Mar 15 | Short-term liquidity impact |
| LTCG harvesting | March | Selling pressure in last 2 weeks |
| Index rebalancing (Nifty) | March, September | Stock-specific, short-term |
| AMFI market cap reclassification | January, July | MF portfolio rebalancing |
| FII tax filing | March 31 | Portfolio cleanup |
| Muhurat trading | Diwali | Sentiment indicator |
| Insurance renewal season | March | Corporate insurance decisions |

## Monthly Data Releases

| Data | Release Date | Impact Level |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| CPI Inflation | 12th-14th of month | High (impacts rate expectations) |
| WPI Inflation | 14th-15th of month | Moderate |
| IIP (Industrial Production) | 12th of month (2-month lag) | Low-Moderate |
| GDP (quarterly) | Feb, May, Aug, Nov (end of month) | High |
| Auto sales | 1st of every month | Moderate |
| GST collection | 1st of every month | Moderate |
| PMI Manufacturing | 1st business day of month | Moderate |
| PMI Services | 3rd-5th business day | Moderate |
| Forex reserves | Weekly (Friday RBI data) | Low |
| FII/DII flows | Daily (provisional), monthly (final) | High |
| Credit growth | Fortnightly (RBI) | Moderate |
| Mutual fund flows | 10th-15th of following month (AMFI) | Moderate |

## Global Events Impacting Indian Markets

| Event | Timing | Impact on India |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| US Fed FOMC meetings | 8 times/year | High — FII flows, USD/INR |
| US Non-Farm Payrolls | 1st Friday of month | Moderate — US recession gauge |
| US CPI data | 10th-14th of month | Moderate — Fed rate path |
| China PMI | 1st of month | Moderate — metals, commodities |
| Crude oil (OPEC meetings) | Quarterly+ | High — India is net importer |
| US earnings (FAANG) | Quarterly | Impacts Nifty IT sentiment |
| BOJ meetings | 8 times/year | Moderate — Yen carry trade unwind risk |
| ECB meetings | 6 times/year | Low-Moderate — Euro flows |
