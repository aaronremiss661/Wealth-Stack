## PE/VC Exit Routes

| Exit Type | Share of Exits | Timeline |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| IPO | 25-30% | 5-8 years |
| Secondary sale (to other PE) | 30-35% | 3-5 years |
| Strategic sale (M&A) | 20-25% | 4-7 years |
| Buyback by promoter | 10-15% | 3-5 years |
| Open market sale (post-IPO) | 5-10% | Lock-in + 1-2 years |

## Investment Metrics

| Metric | Meaning | Good Benchmark (India) |
|--------|---------|----------------------|
| IRR (Gross) | Internal rate of return | > 20% |
| IRR (Net of fees) | After management + carry | > 15% |
| MOIC | Multiple on Invested Capital | > 2.5x |
| DPI | Distributed to Paid-In | > 1.0x (by year 6-7) |
| TVPI | Total Value to Paid-In | > 2.0x |
| J-Curve | Negative returns in early years | Normal — years 1-3 |
