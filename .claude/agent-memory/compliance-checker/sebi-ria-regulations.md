# SEBI Registered Investment Adviser (RIA) Regulations

Source: SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013 (amended 2020)

## Fee Structure Limits

| Fee Model | Maximum Permitted |
|-----------|------------------|
| AUM-based fee | 2.5% per annum of AUM under advice |
| Fixed fee | Rs 1,25,000 per annum per family (across all services) |

- RIA must choose ONE fee model: either AUM-based or fixed fee (not both)
- Fee must be agreed in writing before commencement of services
- No commissions or trail income allowed — RIAs must be fee-only
- Revised limits effective from SEBI circular 2020

## Registration Requirements

| Requirement | Individual RIA | Non-Individual RIA |
|------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Net worth | Rs 5 lakh | Rs 50 lakh |
| Qualification | CFA / CFP / MBA (Finance) / PG in Finance / CA / ICWA / CMA | Principal officer must qualify |
| Experience | 5 years (general) or 2 years (if postgraduate in finance) | Principal officer must qualify |
| NISM Certification | NISM Series X-A & X-B | Principal officer must have |
| Registration fee | Rs 10,000 (5 years) | Rs 5,00,000 (5 years) |

## Compliance Obligations

1. **Client agreement**: Written advisory agreement mandatory before providing advice
2. **Risk profiling**: Must assess client risk tolerance, capacity, and need before advice
3. **Suitability**: Every recommendation must match client's risk profile
4. **Disclosure of conflicts**: Material conflicts of interest must be disclosed
5. **No dual registration**: Cannot be both RIA and mutual fund distributor
6. **Record keeping**: Maintain records for minimum 5 years (10 years for KYC)
7. **Audit**: Annual compliance audit by practicing CA
8. **Grievance redressal**: Must have documented grievance mechanism
9. **Client segregation**: Must maintain client-wise records of advice given
10. **Fee receipt**: Must issue proper receipts/invoices with GST

## KYC Requirements

- CKYC (Central KYC) registration mandatory for all clients
- KRA (KYC Registration Agency) records must be updated
- PAN verification mandatory
- In-person verification (IPV) or video verification required
- Re-KYC every 10 years (low-risk clients) or 2 years (high-risk)

## SEBI Inspection & Enforcement

- SEBI can inspect RIA books and records at any time
- Annual compliance report submission via SEBI intermediary portal
- Penalties: Warning, suspension, cancellation of registration
- SCORES (SEBI Complaints Redress System) — must respond within 30 days

## Mutual Fund Distributor (MFD) vs RIA

| Aspect | MFD | RIA |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Revenue | Commission from AMC (trail) | Fee from client |
| Registration | AMFI (ARN) | SEBI |
| Qualification | NISM Series V-A | NISM X-A & X-B + experience |
| Product scope | Only mutual funds | All financial products |
| Fiduciary duty | Suitability obligation | Fiduciary obligation |
| Can recommend direct plans | No (no commission) | Yes (must recommend direct) |
