# PMS & AIF Regulatory Rules — SEBI

## Portfolio Management Services (PMS)

### Minimum Investment
- **Rs 50,00,000 (50 lakh)** minimum ticket size per client
- Increased from Rs 25 lakh (SEBI circular effective Jan 2020)

### SEBI PMS Regulations Key Points

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Registration | SEBI registered Portfolio Manager |
| Net worth | Rs 5 crore minimum |
| Principal officer | NISM Series XII certification |
| Reporting | Monthly portfolio statement to client |
| Disclosure | Monthly disclosure on SEBI website |
| Benchmark | Must declare benchmark at onboarding |
| Fee disclosure | Performance fee methodology clearly defined |

### PMS Fee Structures
- **Fixed fee**: 1-3% of AUM per annum
- **Performance fee**: Calculated on high-water mark basis (SEBI mandated)
- **Hybrid**: Fixed + performance (most common: 1-1.5% fixed + 10-20% profit share above hurdle)
- Exit load: Typically 1-3% if redeemed within 1-2 years
- No upfront fee permitted

### PMS Disclosure Requirements (SEBI 2024 Guidelines)
- Monthly performance disclosure on SEBI PMS benchmark website
- Must show returns vs benchmark (1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, 3Y, 5Y, since inception)
- Must disclose AUM category-wise
- Complaint statistics on SCORES

## Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)

### Minimum Investment by Category

| Category | Minimum Investment | Minimum Fund Size | Manager Commitment |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Cat I | Rs 1 crore | Rs 20 crore | 2.5% of fund or Rs 5 Cr (whichever lower) |
| Cat II | Rs 1 crore | Rs 20 crore | 2.5% of fund or Rs 5 Cr (whichever lower) |
| Cat III | Rs 1 crore | Rs 20 crore | 5% of fund or Rs 10 Cr (whichever lower) |

- Maximum 1,000 investors per scheme
- Employees/directors/fund managers can invest minimum Rs 25 lakh

### AIF Categories

**Category I** — Receive government/regulatory incentives:
- Venture Capital Funds
- SME Funds
- Social Venture Funds
- Infrastructure Funds

**Category II** — No specific incentives or restrictions (residual category):
- Private Equity Funds
- Debt Funds
- Fund of Funds
- Distressed Asset Funds

**Category III** — Can use leverage and short-selling:
- Hedge Funds
- Long-Short Equity Funds
- PIPE Funds

### AIF Taxation (FY 2025-26)

| Category | Tax Treatment |
|----------|--------------|
| Cat I & II | Pass-through for income (except business income) — taxed in hands of investors |
| Cat III | Fund-level taxation at maximum marginal rate (42.7%) |

- Cat I/II: LTCG at 12.5%, STCG at 20% (equity) — in investor's hands
- Cat III: Fund pays tax, investor receives net-of-tax distributions

### AIF Registration
- Application fee: Rs 1,00,000 (Cat I/II), Rs 1,00,000 (Cat III)
- Registration fee: Rs 5,00,000 (one-time)
- Compliance officer mandatory
- Annual reporting to SEBI via PMR (Private Placement Memorandum Report)
