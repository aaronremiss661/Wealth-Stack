## Key Observations

### Recovery Patterns
- Average recovery time from 20%+ drawdown: 12-18 months
- V-shaped recovery (COVID): Fastest in history — 8 months
- U-shaped recovery (GFC): Slowest — 24 months
- SIP through drawdowns: Rupee cost averaging significantly improves IRR

### Sector Drawdown Behavior
- **Defensives** (Pharma, FMCG): Drawdown typically 50-70% of broad market
- **Cyclicals** (Metals, Infra): Drawdown typically 120-150% of broad market
- **Financials**: Amplified drawdowns during credit events (NBFC crisis, NPAs)
- **IT**: Correlated with global risk-off and USD weakness

## Risk Communication Framework

When discussing drawdowns with clients:
1. Show worst-case scenario for their portfolio composition
2. Translate percentages to rupee amounts (e.g., "Rs 1Cr portfolio could temporarily drop to Rs 65L")
3. Show historical recovery periods to set expectations
4. Emphasize: no permanent loss if portfolio is diversified and held through recovery
5. Stress-test: "If market falls 30%, can you avoid panic selling?"
