## Step-Up SIP Impact

Annual SIP increase of 10% dramatically improves outcomes:

| Base SIP | Duration | Flat SIP (12% return) | 10% Step-Up SIP (12% return) |
|----------|----------|---------------------|------------------------------|
| Rs 10K/month | 15 years | Rs 50.0L | Rs 80.4L |
| Rs 10K/month | 20 years | Rs 98.9L | Rs 188.4L |
| Rs 10K/month | 25 years | Rs 187.9L | Rs 429.4L |
| Rs 25K/month | 20 years | Rs 2.47Cr | Rs 4.71Cr |

Step-up SIP should be aligned with expected salary growth (8-12% annual increment).

## Safe Withdrawal Rate (India Adaptation)

- US 4% rule: Based on US inflation (~2-3%) and bond yields
- India adaptation: 3.0-3.5% withdrawal rate recommended
- Higher inflation (6%) requires higher corpus
- Bucket strategy: 2 years cash + 5 years debt + rest in equity
