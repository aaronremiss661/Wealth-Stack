# Derivatives Basics — Indian Market
**Last updated**: 2025-03-25
**Source**: NSE, SEBI F&O framework
**Affects**: capital-markets-research, risk-profiler

## F&O Market Structure
- Exchange: NSE (primary), BSE (limited)
- Settlement: Cash-settled (no delivery for index), physical delivery for stock options/futures
- Expiry: Weekly (Thursday) for Nifty/Bank Nifty, Monthly (last Thursday) for stocks
- Trading hours: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST

## India VIX
- Measures Nifty 50 option implied volatility (next 30 days)
- Range: 10-15 (calm), 15-20 (moderate), 20-30 (elevated), 30+ (panic)
- Inversely correlated with Nifty — VIX spikes during crashes
- Historical: 90 (Mar 2020 COVID), 50+ (2008 GFC), 10-12 (calm 2023-2024)

## SEBI F&O Regulations (2024 Updates)
- Increased lot size for index derivatives (Nifty lot from 50 → 25 after multiple revisions)
- STT on options selling increased to 0.1% of premium (from 0.0625%)
- Weekly expiry restricted to one benchmark index per exchange
- Upfront margin collection mandatory (peak margin reporting)
- Calendar spread margin benefit only for same-expiry contracts

## Tax on Derivatives
- All F&O income/loss treated as **business income** (not capital gains)
- Can offset against other business income
- Must declare as non-speculative business income (if proper books maintained)
- If turnover >Rs 10Cr: Tax audit mandatory (Section 44AB)
- Turnover calculation: Sum of absolute profit + absolute loss on each trade
