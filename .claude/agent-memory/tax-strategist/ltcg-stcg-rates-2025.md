# Capital Gains Tax Rates — FY 2025-26

Source: Finance Act 2024 (Union Budget July 2024 amendments)

## Equity & Equity Mutual Funds (Listed, STT Paid)

| Type | Holding Period | Tax Rate | Exemption |
|------|---------------|----------|-----------|
| LTCG | > 12 months | 12.5% | First Rs 1.25 lakh per PAN per year exempt |
| STCG | <= 12 months | 20% | No exemption |

- LTCG exemption applies per PAN: Individual + HUF = Rs 2.50 lakh combined strategy
- Surcharge applicable as per income slab (max 15% surcharge on LTCG)
- Health & education cess: 4% on tax + surcharge

## Debt Mutual Funds (Purchased After 1 April 2023)

| Type | Holding Period | Tax Rate | Indexation |
|------|---------------|----------|------------|
| All gains | Any period | Slab rate | No indexation benefit |

- Debt MFs, gold MFs, international MFs, FoFs with <65% equity: all taxed at slab rate
- No distinction between LTCG and STCG for debt funds post-April 2023
- TDS: 20% for NRIs on debt fund redemption

## Gold (Physical, ETF, SGB)

| Instrument | Holding Period for LTCG | LTCG Rate | STCG Rate |
|------------|------------------------|-----------|-----------|
| Physical gold | > 24 months | 12.5% (no indexation) | Slab rate |
| Gold ETF | > 12 months | 12.5% | Slab rate |
| SGB (held to maturity) | 8 years | Exempt | N/A |
| SGB (sold before maturity) | > 12 months | 12.5% | Slab rate |

## Real Estate

| Type | Holding Period for LTCG | LTCG Rate |
|------|------------------------|-----------|
| Property | > 24 months | 12.5% (no indexation) |

- Post Budget 2024: No indexation benefit for property LTCG
- Grandfathering: Properties acquired before 23 July 2024 can use old rate (20% with indexation) if it results in lower tax
- Section 54: Reinvestment in one residential property (within 2 years purchase / 3 years construction)
- Section 54EC: Invest up to Rs 50 lakh in NHAI/REC bonds (5-year lock-in)

## Securities Transaction Tax (STT) Rates

| Transaction | STT Rate |
|-------------|----------|
| Equity delivery (buy + sell) | 0.1% each side |
| Equity intraday (sell) | 0.025% |
| Futures (sell) | 0.02% (increased from 0.0125%) |
| Options (sell/exercise) | 0.1% on premium (increased from 0.0625%) |
| MF equity redemption | 0.001% |

Note: STT rates were increased in Budget 2024 for F&O transactions.

## Dividend Taxation

- Dividends taxed at slab rate in hands of investor (since FY 2020-21)
- TDS at 10% if dividend exceeds Rs 5,000/year per company
- Section 80M: Deduction for inter-corporate dividends (holding companies)

## Key Planning Points

1. Harvest LTCG up to Rs 1.25L annually to reset cost basis (tax-free)
2. Hold equity positions past 12 months to get 12.5% vs 20% rate
3. Debt fund gains now at slab rate — consider FDs or debt instruments for comparison
4. Use HUF PAN for additional Rs 1.25L LTCG exemption
5. SGB held to maturity is the most tax-efficient gold instrument (zero tax on gains)
