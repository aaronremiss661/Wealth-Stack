# SEBI BRSR Framework — Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting

## Overview

- **Mandated by**: SEBI (effective FY 2022-23)
- **Applicable to**: Top 1,000 listed companies by market capitalization
- **Replaces**: Business Responsibility Report (BRR)
- **Filing**: Part of annual report, filed with stock exchanges

## BRSR Core (Enhanced Disclosure — FY 2023-24 onwards)

BRSR Core is a subset of BRSR with quantifiable, assured ESG metrics.

### Applicability of BRSR Core
- Top 150 companies by market cap: Must get reasonable assurance from FY 2024-25
- Top 250 companies: Extended from FY 2025-26
- Must also get assurance on value chain (top suppliers/customers)

## Key ESG Metrics in BRSR

### Environmental (E)
| Metric | What to Report |
|--------|---------------|
| GHG emissions | Scope 1 (direct) + Scope 2 (indirect from energy) in tCO2e |
| Energy consumption | Total energy (GJ), renewable %, intensity ratios |
| Water consumption | Total withdrawal, recycled water %, intensity |
| Waste management | Hazardous and non-hazardous waste, recycling rates |
| Biodiversity | Impact on ecologically sensitive areas |

### Social (S)
| Metric | What to Report |
|--------|---------------|
| Employee diversity | Gender ratio, differently-abled, median salary ratio (M/F) |
| Health & safety | LTIFR, fatalities, health insurance coverage |
| Human rights | Child labor policy, forced labor, complaints mechanism |
| Community | CSR spending, community development programs |
| Supply chain | Supplier labor practices, assessment frequency |

### Governance (G)
| Metric | What to Report |
|--------|---------------|
| Board diversity | Gender, independence, meeting attendance |
| Anti-corruption | Complaints, convictions, policy existence |
| Data privacy | Cyber incidents, data breach disclosures |
| Tax transparency | Tax paid by jurisdiction |
| Related party | RPT details, board approval for material RPTs |

## SEBI-Recognized ESG Rating Providers (ERPs)

SEBI has mandated that ESG ratings must come from SEBI-registered ERPs:

| Provider | Status |
|----------|--------|
| CRISIL | Registered |
| ICRA | Registered |
| CARE | Registered |
| Acuite | Registered |
| Infomerics | Registered |
| S&P Global (via CRISIL) | International partner |
| MSCI | International (not SEBI-registered directly) |
| Sustainalytics | International |

### ERP Regulations (SEBI, July 2023)
- Must be registered with SEBI
- Transparent methodology required
- Must disclose data sources and weightages
- Subscriber/issuer conflicts to be managed
- Comply with IOSCO recommendations

## Carbon Credits — Basics

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Carbon credit | 1 credit = 1 tonne CO2 equivalent avoided/reduced |
| Indian Carbon Market | Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) framework, 2023 |
| Compliance market | Mandatory for obligated entities (energy-intensive industries) |
| Voluntary market | Companies buying credits for net-zero commitments |
| Price (India, voluntary) | Rs 500-2,000 per tCO2e (early market, evolving) |
| Global reference (EU ETS) | EUR 60-80 per tCO2e |

### India Carbon Credit Trading Scheme (CCTS) — 2023
- Notified under Energy Conservation Act, 2001 (amended 2022)
- Grid-connected entities and large industries as obligated entities
- BEE as administrator
- CERC as market regulator
- Trading on power exchanges (IEX, PXIL)
