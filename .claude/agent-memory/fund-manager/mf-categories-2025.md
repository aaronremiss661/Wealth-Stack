# SEBI Mutual Fund Categories — FY 2025-26

Source: SEBI Circular on Categorization and Rationalization of MF Schemes (Oct 2017, updated)

## Equity Fund Categories

| Category | Equity Allocation Rule | Universe | Benchmark |
|----------|----------------------|----------|-----------|
| Large Cap | Min 80% in top 100 stocks | Top 100 by market cap | Nifty 50 / Nifty 100 |
| Mid Cap | Min 65% in 101-250 stocks | 101st to 250th by market cap | Nifty Midcap 150 |
| Small Cap | Min 65% in 251+ stocks | 251st and below | Nifty Smallcap 250 |
| Large & Mid Cap | Min 35% each in large + mid | Top 250 | Nifty LargeMidcap 250 |
| Multi Cap | Min 25% each in large/mid/small | All caps | Nifty 500 |
| Flexi Cap | Min 65% equity, no cap constraints | All caps (fund manager discretion) | Nifty 500 |
| ELSS (Tax Saver) | Min 80% equity | All caps | Nifty 500 |
| Focused Fund | Max 30 stocks, min 65% equity | Specified cap range | Category-specific |
| Sectoral / Thematic | Min 80% in specified sector/theme | Sector stocks | Sector index |
| Value / Contra | Min 65% equity, value/contrarian style | All caps | Nifty 500 Value 50 |
| Dividend Yield | Min 65% in dividend-yielding stocks | All caps | Nifty Dividend Opp 50 |
