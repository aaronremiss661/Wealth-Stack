## Age-Based Allocation Rule of Thumb

- **Equity allocation = 100 - Age** (traditional)
- **Indian adaptation = 110 - Age** (higher growth economy, longer horizon)
- Adjust for: risk tolerance, income stability, dependents, existing assets

| Age Group | Equity | Debt | Others |
|-----------|--------|------|--------|
| 25-35 | 70-85% | 10-20% | 5-10% |
| 35-45 | 55-70% | 20-30% | 5-15% |
| 45-55 | 40-55% | 35-45% | 5-15% |
| 55-65 | 25-40% | 45-60% | 10-15% |
| 65+ | 15-25% | 60-70% | 10-15% |

## Rebalancing Triggers

| Method | Rule | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|------|
| Calendar | Rebalance every 6/12 months | Simple, disciplined | May miss extremes |
| Threshold | Rebalance when any asset drifts >5% from target | Responsive | More transactions |
| Hybrid | Calendar check + threshold override | Best of both | Slightly complex |
