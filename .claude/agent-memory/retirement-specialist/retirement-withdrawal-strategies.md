# Retirement Withdrawal Strategies — India Adapted

## Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) for India

### Why 4% Rule Doesn't Work for India
- US 4% rule assumes: 2-3% inflation, 60/40 portfolio, 30-year horizon
- India has: 6% inflation, different asset class returns, higher equity volatility

### India-Adapted SWR

| Scenario | SWR | Required Corpus (Rs 1L/month expenses) |
|----------|-----|---------------------------------------|
| Conservative (very safe) | 3.0% | Rs 4.00 Cr |
| Moderate (recommended) | 3.5% | Rs 3.43 Cr |
| Slightly aggressive | 4.0% | Rs 3.00 Cr |
| Aggressive (higher risk of depletion) | 4.5% | Rs 2.67 Cr |

**Recommendation**: Use 3.0-3.5% SWR for Indian retirees due to higher inflation.

## Bucket Strategy (Recommended for India)

### Three-Bucket Framework

**Bucket 1 — Cash / Liquidity (2-3 Years of Expenses)**
| Instrument | Allocation | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Savings account | 3 months | Immediate access |
| Liquid fund | 6 months | Near-cash, better returns than savings |
| Ultra-short duration fund | 6 months | Slightly higher yield |
| FD (laddered, 6-12 months) | 12 months | Stable, predictable |
| **Total Bucket 1** | **24-36 months expenses** | **No market risk** |

**Bucket 2 — Income / Stability (3-7 Years of Expenses)**
| Instrument | Allocation | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Short duration fund | 25% | Moderate duration risk |
| Corporate bond fund | 25% | Yield pickup |
| Conservative hybrid fund | 25% | Small equity kicker |
| SCSS / POMIS | 25% | Guaranteed government returns |
| **Total Bucket 2** | **3-7 years expenses** | **Low-to-moderate risk** |

**Bucket 3 — Growth (Remaining Corpus)**
| Instrument | Allocation | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Large cap / index fund | 40% | Growth with stability |
| Flexi cap fund | 25% | Multi-cap growth |
| Balanced advantage fund | 20% | Dynamic equity-debt |
| Gold (SGB/ETF) | 15% | Inflation hedge, diversification |
| **Total Bucket 3** | **Remainder** | **Growth to beat inflation** |

### Bucket Refilling Rules
1. Annually, review Bucket 1 and top up from Bucket 2 (if Bucket 1 < 18 months)
2. If equity markets up 15%+, rebalance from Bucket 3 to Bucket 2
3. If equity markets down 20%+, do NOT sell from Bucket 3 — use Bucket 1 & 2
4. Every 3 years, strategic rebalancing across all buckets

## SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) Strategies

### Fixed-Amount SWP
- Withdraw fixed rupee amount monthly from MF
- Simple, predictable cash flow
- Risk: Depletes faster in falling markets (sequence-of-returns risk)
- Best from: Balanced advantage or conservative hybrid fund

### Variable-Rate SWP (Guardrails)
| Market Condition | Withdrawal Rule |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Normal (portfolio return > 5%) | Withdraw base amount + inflation adjustment |
| Below average (return 0-5%) | Withdraw base amount only (no inflation raise) |
| Bear market (return < 0%) | Cut withdrawal by 10-15% from base |
| Bull market (return > 15%) | Take extra 5-10% for discretionary/reserve |

### SWP Tax Optimization
- SWP from equity fund (>1 year): Only gains portion taxed at 12.5% LTCG
- In a Rs 10,000 SWP, if only Rs 2,000 is gain, only Rs 2,000 is taxable
- FIFO method applies: Oldest units redeemed first
- Annual LTCG exemption: Rs 1.25L per year — plan SWP to stay within this
