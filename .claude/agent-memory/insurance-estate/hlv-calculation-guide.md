# Human Life Value (HLV) Calculation Guide

## What is HLV?

Human Life Value represents the economic value of a person to their dependents — the total financial impact if that person were to pass away. It determines the minimum life insurance coverage needed.

## HLV Calculation Methods

### Method 1: Income Replacement Method (Simple)

**Formula**: HLV = Annual Income x Remaining Working Years x Adjustment Factor

| Component | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Current annual income (post-tax) | A |
| Years to retirement | N |
| Income growth rate | g (typically 8-10%) |
| Discount rate | r (typically 7-8%) |
| Adjustment factor | (1+g)/(1+r) compounded over N years |

**Simplified Rule of Thumb**:
- Age 25-35: HLV = 15-20x annual income
- Age 35-45: HLV = 12-15x annual income
- Age 45-55: HLV = 8-12x annual income
- Age 55+: HLV = 5-8x annual income

### Method 2: Expense Replacement Method (Comprehensive)

**Steps**:
1. Estimate annual family expenses (excluding own expenses, ~70% of household)
2. Add annual loan EMIs and liabilities
3. Add future lumpy expenses (children's education, wedding)
4. Subtract existing financial assets (investments, insurance, EPF)
5. Calculate present value of stream using discount rate

## Practical Examples

### Example 1: 32-Year-Old IT Professional
- Annual income: Rs 20L (post-tax ~Rs 16L)
- Family expenses: Rs 10L/year
- Home loan: Rs 40L outstanding
- Child education (2 kids): Rs 1Cr (future value, PV ~Rs 40L)
- Existing cover: Rs 50L (employer group) + Rs 25L (personal)
- EPF + investments: Rs 30L

**HLV Calculation**:
- Expense stream (25 years x 18.9): Rs 10L x 18.9 = Rs 1.89Cr
- Loans: Rs 40L
- Education: Rs 40L
- Total need: Rs 2.69Cr
- Less existing: Rs 1.05Cr
- **Insurance gap: Rs 1.64Cr → Buy Rs 1.5-2Cr term plan**

### Example 2: 45-Year-Old Business Owner
- Annual income: Rs 50L (post-tax ~Rs 35L)
- Family expenses: Rs 20L/year
- Business loans with personal guarantee: Rs 1Cr
- Child education (1 kid, college in 3 years): Rs 50L
- Existing cover: Rs 1Cr term + Rs 50L group
- Investments + property: Rs 3Cr

**HLV Calculation**:
- Expense stream (20 years x 16.2): Rs 20L x 16.2 = Rs 3.24Cr
- Loans: Rs 1Cr
- Education: Rs 50L
- Total need: Rs 4.74Cr
- Less existing: Rs 4.5Cr
- **Insurance gap: Rs 24L → Current cover adequate, review in 2 years**

## HLV Review Triggers

- Marriage / new dependent
- Child birth
- Home purchase / new loan
- Significant salary increase (>30%)
- Starting a business
- Death of a co-earning spouse
- Every 3 years as routine review
