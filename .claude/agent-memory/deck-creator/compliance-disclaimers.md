regulatory requirements as mutual funds. There is no assurance that the

## Risk Disclosure Statements

### Equity Risk Disclosure
```
Equity investments are subject to market risk, including the risk of loss of
principal. Historical returns of equity markets (including Nifty 50, Nifty
Midcap 150, etc.) do not guarantee future performance. Equity investments
are suitable for investors with a long-term horizon (5+ years) and appropriate
risk tolerance.
```

### Debt / Fixed Income Risk Disclosure
```
Debt investments are subject to interest rate risk, credit risk, and liquidity
risk. The NAV of debt mutual funds may fluctuate based on changes in interest
rates and credit quality of underlying securities. Debt investments are not
risk-free and can result in loss of principal.
```

### F&O Risk Disclosure
```
Trading in derivatives (futures and options) involves substantial risk of loss
and is not suitable for all investors. The use of leverage in derivatives trading
can magnify both profits and losses. 9 out of 10 individual traders in the
equity F&O segment incurred net losses (SEBI study, January 2023).
```

### NRI-Specific Disclaimer
```
NRI investments are subject to FEMA regulations and RBI guidelines. Repatriation
of funds is subject to applicable tax deduction at source and regulatory approvals.
Investors should be aware of the tax implications in both India and their country
of residence. DTAA benefits are subject to provision of Tax Residency Certificate.
```
